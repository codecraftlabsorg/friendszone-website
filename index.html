Our Story

Where Every Meal Is a
Memory Worth Making

Friends Zone was born from a simple belief — that great food should be accessible to everyone. Nestled in the heart of Hayaghat, Bihar, we've been serving the community with love, authenticity, and flavours that keep you coming back. From our legendary Chicken Dum Biryani to freshly made dosas every morning, every dish is crafted with care.
